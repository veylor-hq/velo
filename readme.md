# Velo: The Ultimate Car Tracking App
Velo is designed to bridge the gap between heavy, spreadsheet-like maintenance logs and the rapid-entry needs of a daily driver

## Features
### Refueling & Fuel Economy
Rapid Entry: Optimised for "at-the-pump" logging with minimal taps with odometer tracking
Efficiency Analytics: Automated calculation of L/100km, MPG, and cost-per-mile metrics.
Fuel Stations: Geolocation-aware tagging to track which fuel brands give your car better performance

### Mods, Service & Maintenance
Detailed Service Logs: Track oil changes, filter replacements, and major repairs with high-resolution photo attachments if needed.
Preventative Reminders: Set time or mileage-based triggers for upcoming maintenance (e.g., "Change oil every 10k miles", or "every 6 months").
Recurring Expenses: Track non-mechanical costs like Insurance, Road Tax, MOT, Fines, or even parking fees.

Parts Catalog: Store specific part numbers (e.g., spark plug codes, oil filter models) so you never have to look them up twice.

### Advanced Automotive Intelligence
Cost of Ownership: Visual breakdown of total spend per vehicle, categorized by fuel, service, and upgrades.
Sentinel Planner: A complex maintenance forecast system that predicts when parts will wear out based on your driving habits.


### Mobile-First Experience
Lock Screen Widgets: One-tap access to log a refuel or view remaining miles until your next service.
Telegram Sync: Receive maintenance reminders and weekly spend summaries directly via the Telegram bot.
Offline Mode: Log data in underground garages or remote areas; syncs automatically when you're back online.

### Data & Portability
LubeLogger Import/Export: Fully compatible CSV/JSON structures for seamless migration from LubeLogger.
Multi-Vehicle Support: Manage an entire fleet from a single account with per-vehicle permission sharing.
